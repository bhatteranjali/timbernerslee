Hi!

Time Berners Lee is a software engineer who invented the Internet or the World Wide Web in 1989 while at CERn, the European Laboratory for Particle Physics.

He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford.

In 2001, he became a Fellow of the Royal Society and in 2004 he was knighted.

In 1994, he founded the World Wide Web Consortium(W3C) at the Laboratory of Computer Science(LCS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Tecnology in Boston.It was made of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards and also improve the Web. It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalties and patents.Berners Lee has served as the director of this W3C since it was founded

In 1995,Microsoft created the first version of Internet Explorer and this commercial web browser created enormous growth in websites and web users.

The Web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
